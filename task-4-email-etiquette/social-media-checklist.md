## Do’s

1. Think before you post\*\* – Make sure your content is respectful and appropriate.
2. Protect your privacy\*\* – Adjust privacy settings and limit personal information shared.
3. Verify information\*\* – Check facts before sharing news or posts.
4. Maintain a professional image\*\* – Remember that future employers may view your profile.
5. Be respectful and kind\*\* – Engage in positive and constructive interactions.

## Don’ts

1. Don’t share sensitive personal details\*\* – Avoid posting your address, phone number, or passwords.
2. Don’t spread misinformation\*\* – Avoid sharing unverified or fake content.
3. Don’t engage in online arguments or bullying\*\* – It can harm your reputation and others.
4. Don’t post inappropriate content\*\* – Offensive or explicit posts can have long-term consequences.
5. Don’t overshare your daily life\*\* – Too much information can compromise your safety and privacy.
